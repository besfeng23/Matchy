# Growth + Retention Ethics

Rules:
- Growth copy must be clear, factual, and consent-aware.
- Respect suppression, frequency caps, and channel preferences.
- Do not use misleading urgency or unsupported claims.
- Pandora may recommend candidates, but delivery still requires policy checks.

Done:
- Consent and suppression are checked.
- Copy is reviewable.
- Metrics and stop conditions exist.
