# Product Truth + Compliance

Rules:
- Preserve disclosed operator-assisted workflow in data, UI, checkout, and terms.
- Show action cost before confirmation.
- Do not promise unsupported outcomes.
- Keep report, block, refund, cancellation, and audit paths intact.

Done:
- Disclosure exists where needed.
- Paid actions are clear.
- Sensitive actions are logged.
- Policy copy is updated when behavior changes.
