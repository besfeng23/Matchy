# Growth + Retention Ethics

Use before editing growth or retention systems.

Rules:
- Growth copy must be clear, factual, and consent-aware.
- Respect suppression, frequency caps, and channel preferences.
- Do not use misleading urgency or claims the product cannot support.
- Pandora may recommend candidates, but delivery still requires policy checks.
- Track complaints, opt-outs, refunds, and conversion quality.

Checks:
- What user state triggers this?
- What channel is allowed?
- What frequency cap applies?
- Is the message factual?
- What metric could show harm or poor quality?

Done:
- Consent and suppression are checked.
- Copy is reviewable.
- Metrics and stop conditions exist.
