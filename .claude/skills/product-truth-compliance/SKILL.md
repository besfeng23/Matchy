# Product Truth + Compliance Skill

## Mission
Keep Matchy accurate, transparent, and reviewable. Build disclosed operator-assisted communication workflows with clear billing and user controls.

## Non-negotiable rules
- Operator-assisted profiles and conversations must remain disclosed in data, UI, checkout, and terms.
- Show credit cost before a paid action.
- Do not promise outcomes the product cannot actually deliver.
- Preserve report, block, refund, cancellation, and data-rights flows.
- Preserve audit logs for moderator, admin, billing, profile, and policy changes.

## Required checks
- What user expectation could this feature create?
- Is the disclosure visible before spend?
- Is profile type stored and queryable?
- Does checkout state what is being purchased?
- Does the feature increase refund, complaint, or chargeback risk?

## Acceptance criteria
- Required disclosures are present.
- Paid actions are clear before confirmation.
- Sensitive actions are audit-logged.
- Policy pages or copy are updated if behavior changes.

## Red flags
- UI-only disclosure with no database truth.
- Subscription or credit terms that are unclear.
- Missing report, cancel, refund, or audit path.
